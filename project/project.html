WHO AM I?



A writer with a dilemated heart and weird mood swings. It's the mood which decides my vibes. I don't run away from attention, but it depends on the crowd I am in. In a lot of situations, I'm a dilemated soul who is just quietly observing.Like extroverts, I enjoy conversation — but, like introverts, I hate small talk. Speaking of limits to my social comfort zone, I am comfortable socializing. I really enjoy meeting new people, but I prefer to have my besties around me when I do it. I'm unlikely to run up and introduce myself to a complete stranger, I'm very bad at initiating talks with a stranger. I'd enjoy entire weekend alone rather than go out partying. I love to find myself in virtual complex situations and search for a clue to get back in terms.
